# Xinke ICT Competition

The **National College Students’ New Generation Information and Communication Technology Competition** (大学生新一代信息通信科技大赛, widely known as **信科赛**) is a national ICT contest in China. It is co-sponsored by industry and association partners (including the MIIT Talent Exchange Center and China Communications Enterprise Association) and appears on the China Association of Higher Education’s watch list of influential university competitions.

## What participants do

Typical tracks emphasize **5G/5G-A/6G concepts**, wireless engineering practice, embedded and RF labs, and **AI + communications** scenarios. Formats often blend online theory, simulation, and hands-on engineering tasks rather than a single exam.

## Why it matters

- Strong alignment with telecom and embedded hiring profiles  
- Cross-disciplinary: signals, networking, Python/MATLAB, and hardware  
- Clear progression from provincial rounds to a national final
