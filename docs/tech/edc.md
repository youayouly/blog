# National Undergraduate Electronic Design Contest

The **National Undergraduate Electronic Design Contest** (全国大学生电子设计竞赛) is a biennial competition in China for university students in electrical and related majors. It is organized under the Ministry of Education’s guidance and stresses **end-to-end analog/digital design**, embedded firmware, and **time-boxed prototyping** (often a multi-day “sealed” build window followed by test and interview).

## Typical focus areas

- Power supplies, amplifiers, filters, and mixed-signal front ends  
- MCU/FPGA-based instruments: acquisition, control, and human interface  
- Sensors, signal conditioning, and real-time algorithms  
- System integration, reliability, and measurement against a published spec  

## Skills you exercise

- Schematic and PCB iteration, SPICE or equivalent validation  
- C on MCUs, DMA/ADC timing, and debugging with scopes/logic analyzers  
- Written design reports and live demonstration to judges
